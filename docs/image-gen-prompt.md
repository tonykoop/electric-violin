The instrument is recognizably a violin in scale and proportion but its body is a minimal solid plank silhouette rather than a hollow acoustic shell — a sculpted skeletal hardwood body with no soundholes, no top plate, no fragile bouts, just enough mass to mount the bridge, tailpiece, and electronics. ## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension lines or measurement callouts; no hollow acoustic soundbox or f-holes; no impossible wiring or floating mechanisms; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
