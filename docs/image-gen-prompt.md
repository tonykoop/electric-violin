# Image Generation Prompt

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 studio product photograph of a finished solid-body electric violin prototype with explicit 4-string and 5-string design cues: a slim hardwood or laminated body, violin-scale neck, piezo bridge pickup, compact tailpiece, chin rest and shoulder-rest interface, shielded electronics cavity access panel, side-mounted output jack, and clean cable strain relief. The body should look ergonomic and structurally stiff rather than acoustic-hollow, with satin natural wood and dark hardware, neutral studio background, realistic violin scale cues, soft directional studio lighting, about a 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic image of the electric violin being tested in a quiet studio corner, a player holding it in normal violin posture with anatomically plausible hands, a small preamp and cable on a bench, and a notebook for piezo output and balance notes. Natural light, no stage glamour, focus on setup, shoulder/chin interface, bridge pickup, and wiring service access.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of the piezo bridge and electronics transition on the electric violin: strings crossing the bridge, pickup lead exiting cleanly into a shielded cavity, tailpiece and fine tuners visible, satin wood body surface, tiny strain-relief clip, shallow depth of field.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, no watermarks, no impossible floating bridge, no acoustic violin f-holes unless purely decorative and nonfunctional, no extra limbs, anatomically plausible hands if shown.
