# Open String Drone and D Major Scale

**Provenance:** Original exercise.

**How to use:**
- Bars 1–2: bow open D and A strings slowly. Listen: is your tone centred and
  clear through the amp? Adjust gain and bow pressure until it is.
- Bars 3–6: D major scale ascending and descending. One quarter note per bow.
- Bars 7–8: return to open A drone to settle the hand.

**Electric-specific tip:**
- If you hear a harsh edge on the attack of each note, you're using too much
  bow pressure. Reduce weight and increase bow speed slightly.
- The amp reveals every detail of bow technique. Use this exercise to diagnose
  your sound, then correct it.
