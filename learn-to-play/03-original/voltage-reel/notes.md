# Voltage Reel

**Provenance:** Original composition by Heifer Zephyr for this electric violin
build. MIT licensed — free to perform, record, and arrange.

**Designed for:** Tony Koop's electric violin (GDAE standard tuning, first position).

**Range:** E4–G5. Stays in first position; the G5 is the 3rd finger on E string.

**Form:** AABB.

**Character:** A driving reel with a slight Celtic flavour, designed to show off
the electric violin's sustain on the long notes and its precision on the fast
passages.

**Amp settings suggestion:**
- Clean channel, slight overdrive (gain 2–3/10 max)
- Add a touch of delay (150ms, one repeat) for the long held notes in bars 8
  and 16 — it gives the "voltage" shimmer the title implies.

**Practice tips:**
- The F♯ (^F) is the characteristic Dorian note — make it count.
- At ♩=120 with a straight 8th-note feel this is a proper reel. Start at ♩=80.
- The peak G5 in bar 14 (g4) should be the loudest moment — build to it.
